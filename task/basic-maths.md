## Greatest Common Divisor \(GCD\)

Lets consider 6 & 10.

6 = 2 \* 3 and 10 = 2 \* 5. So GCD = 2.

36 = 2 \* 2 \* 3 \* 3 and 60 = 2 \* 2 \* 3 \* 5. So GCD = 2 \* 2 \* 3 = 12
